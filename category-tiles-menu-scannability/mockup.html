Category Tiles — Menu Scannability

Control mirrors today's live mweb menu: a horizontal strip of text-only category pills. Variant replaces it with a rail of visual category tiles (image + label + item count) at the top of the menu, so guests can scan and tap-to-jump to a section instead of scrolling a long item list. Example brand: Talkin' Tacos (13-category menu — the fleet median).

CONTROL Today — text pill nav
Category navigation is a single horizontal strip of text-only pills. No imagery, low scan weight; on a 13-category menu most categories sit off-screen to the right.
